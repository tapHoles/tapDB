Information on the Online Resolution of Disputes

The EU Commission is providing an Internet platform for the extrajudicial settlement of disputes (the “ODR platform”). The ODR platform should serve as the main center for the extrajudicial settlement of disputes, i.e. the contractual obligations concerning, inter alia, online purchase contracts and service agreements. The ODR platform can be accessed via the following link: http://ec.europa.eu/consumers/odr.

For questions relating to the online resolution of disputes, please contact: odr@bigpoint.net

Information on an alternative dispute resolution procedure

Please note that we do not participate in voluntary alternative dispute resolution under the German Consumer Dispute Resolution Act (Verbraucherstreitbeilegungsgesetz, VSBG).
