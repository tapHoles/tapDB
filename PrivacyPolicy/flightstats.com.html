Scraping Policy

Thank you for your interest in FlightStats.

Pursuant to the Terms of Use, scraping our site is forbidden. Our data collection efforts represent a significant and on-going investment that requires compensation from our developer community to be sustainable and to fund continuous improvement.

Our FlightStats Flex APIs with improved functionality, local language support, pricing advantages, and convenient payment options are available for immediate use at: https://developer.flightstats.com/

These services are not free. However, users can access our data in a sustainable manner. To learn more about our services, feel free to Contact us. Our apologies if you have received this message and are not among the many who are harvesting data from our Web properties.
